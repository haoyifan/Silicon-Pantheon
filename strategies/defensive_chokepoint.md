# Defensive Chokepoint

## Doctrine
Hold terrain, attrit the enemy, win by elimination. Do not chase.

## Unit priorities
- **Knight:** take the most defensible central tile (forest or fort) by turn 3 and hold.
- **Archer:** stay behind the knight; shoot whatever approaches.
- **Cavalry:** reserve — only engage when we can kill an exposed enemy in one round without dying to counter.
- **Mage:** heal the knight every time it's below 60% HP.

## Target priorities
1. Enemy cavalry (highest tempo threat).
2. Enemy mage (can heal their line).
3. Whatever is closest to our knight.

## Map heuristics
- Forests give +2 DEF; forts give +3 DEF/RES and heal 3/turn. Stand on them.
- Do not leave the fort unless you can kill something and return.

## Endgame
If the enemy has ≤ 2 units and both are damaged, push with the cavalry for the kill.
Never attempt a seize unless the enemy fort is undefended for 2 full turns.
