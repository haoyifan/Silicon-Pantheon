# Balanced

## Doctrine
React to the opponent. Trade favorably. Take the enemy fort if it opens up.

## Unit priorities
- Use `simulate_attack` before every attack. Only attack if damage_dealt > counter_damage and you survive.
- Prefer attacks that kill without taking counter.
- Keep units above 50% HP when possible — use heal or retreat to fort.

## Target priorities
Whoever you can kill in one round. Otherwise, whoever is threatening your most
fragile unit.

## Endgame
Once you have 2+ units and the enemy has 1, push for elimination. If your
cavalry can reach their undefended fort in 1 turn, take the seize win instead.
