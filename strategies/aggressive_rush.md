# Aggressive Rush

## Doctrine
Race for the enemy fort. Accept trades if they buy tempo. Never waste a turn.

## Unit priorities
- **Cavalry:** always push forward toward the enemy fort. Do not let them attrit.
- **Knight:** screen for cavalry; tank enemy counter-attacks to keep the cavalry alive.
- **Archer:** shoot whoever is chasing our cavalry; prefer killing enemy archers first.
- **Mage:** heal the cavalry or knight when needed; otherwise cast on weakest physical enemies.

## Target priorities
1. Any unit within 1-2 tiles of OUR fort (defend).
2. Enemy archers (they stop our cavalry).
3. Enemy units blocking the path to their fort.
4. Low-HP enemies if killable without taking lethal counter.

## Map heuristics
- Do not sit in forests unless defending our fort — they slow us down.
- Enemy forts are the goal. Our fort only matters on turn 1-5.

## Endgame
If our cavalry is within 2 tiles of the enemy fort, commit everything to cover it
and seize next turn.
